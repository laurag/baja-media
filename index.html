Reaching Crossborders Market

The Baja Media Mission

Offer San Diego marketers the opportunity to advertise their products/services with TV Azteca baja california at affordable rates in Tijuana, Mexico (population 1.6 million) where over 100,000 people cross daily into San Diego representing a purchasing power in the US of $2.5 billion annually.

San Diego/Tijuana Dynamics

  • A large number crossing the border into San Diego return home to Tijuana the same day
  • During weekends Tijuana residents visit San Diego to make and/or for R&R purposes
  • Living in Tijuana and working in San Diego provides workers with the best of both worlds by earning wages in dollars and payig basic living expenses in Mexican pesos.

TV Azteca Network in México

  • TV Azteca is the second largest TV media network in México founded in 1993.
  • Owner of two television nationwide channels in México (Channel 7 and Channel 13). Present in 14 countries including the USA (as Azteca América).
